About Assembly

You might have assembly course.
I had trouble learning the language to begin with, and found that a lot of assembly tutorials didn’t really help. In fact, hardly any of them explained it from a beginner’s point of view, and were extremely vague.


There was only one tutorial I found that really pointed me in the right direction, and it was a guide @tempestatorres5243/, though his tutorial has a few holes in places which makes it sort of impractical. So, this website here is an attempt at help an extreme beginner’s level, to help steer away from all of those vague guides out there. If you have any questions to ask, or any advice or feedback to share, then feel free to contact me.

Services

Consultation
Meeting with the task's specifics, whether it's a course project, laboratory work, or another type of task. It involves checking and gethering methodological materials, teacher or project supervisor recommendations, meeting with development environment, and the rest of toolset. Send the methodological materials for review to get clear understanding of the overall picture and productive meeting. During the first meeting, it's desirable to establish deadlines and payment methods.

Labs
Laboratory work includes writing functional code according to the requirements specified in the methodological materials and by the student, preparing a report, and providing code explanations.

Course Projects
A course project involves writing functional code in accordance with the requirements in the methodological materials and by the student, preparing a report, and providing explanations of the code. Typically, a course project goes with an explanatory note and a significant amount of code, which usually requires more time than laboratory work.

Pair Programming
Sometimes students find it comfortable to observe the coding process because it allows them to ask questions or clarify important aspects of programming during the meeting. This is a convenient and effective method to meet with the situation closer and prepare yourself for exam or any other defense.

Tutoring
Classic lessons in assembly programming. A key aspect of programming is practical exercises. Theory takes up no more than 10% of the lesson. For a big fan of computer science.
